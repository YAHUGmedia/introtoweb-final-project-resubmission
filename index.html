These are three dimensional objects. The object is called a torus. It was created using a Javascript library called p5.js and WebGL. The objects are meant to a be kinetic, rotational study to help me understand creative programming. The textures on these objects were made using Deforum Stable Diffusion v0.5 in a Google Colab Notebook.

A gallery of videos used in the project as textures for the 3D models is available by scrolling down and by clicking on the thumbnails to get a larger view.
